Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect the personal information you provide on our website.

Information We Collect

We collect user names, email addresses when users register or log in to our platform. Additionally, we store information about the friends users add within the platform and details of expenses and transactions.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Facilitate user authentication and authorization.
  • Enable users to add and connect with friends.
  • Record and manage user-generated expenses and transactions.

Data Security

We take security measures to protect your personal information. User passwords are hashed before storage, and access to the data is restricted to authorized personnel within our organization.

Data Sharing

We do not share user data with external parties outside our organization. User information, including friends' data and transaction details, is kept confidential.

Policy Changes

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Users will be notified of significant changes, and we encourage you to review this policy periodically.
